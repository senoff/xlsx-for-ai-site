Free tools for your spreadsheets.

Pick what you need

  • What's inside this Excel file? — a plain-language overview of every sheet and column before you dig in.
  • Why are my formulas showing errors? — find the #REF!, #DIV/0! and other broken formulas in your workbook.
  • Convert Excel to CSV? — turn a sheet into a clean .csv any tool can read.
  • How do I remove duplicate rows? — drop the repeated rows and keep the first of each.
  • Does this Excel file have macros? Is it safe? — check for VBA macros and links to other files before you open or share it.
  • What changed between two Excel files? — see every cell that changed, was added, or was removed between two versions.
  • How do I fix broken links to other files? — freeze references to moved or deleted workbooks so your cells stop breaking.
  • How do I clean up a messy export? — trim spaces, fix numbers stored as text, drop duplicate rows, and standardize inconsistent values.
  • How do I summarize or total up my data? — get the total, average and range of every numeric column, and the counts of every column.
  • How do I remove personal data before sharing? — mask emails, phone numbers, IDs and card/bank numbers, and download a safe copy.
  • How do I pull out just the rows I need? — keep the rows that match your conditions (AND), sort them, and download the result as CSV.

More tools are on the way — get a workbook share-ready in one pass, and more.

How these tools work

Each tool runs the same function your AI agent would call through our API — no reformatting, no surprises. Prefer to skip the upload entirely? Install xlsx-for-ai and ask Claude or Cursor to do any of this on your own machine, with no file ever leaving it.
