What is the #CALC! error in Excel — and how do I fix it?

Seeing this message? It's more specific than it looks — and usually a quick fix. Drop your file in below and it'll be checked for you, no reading required.

fix-formula-errors · xlsx-for-ai

What this message means

This error points at a specific problem in the file, not at your account — which means it's something you can fix and re-upload.

Why it happens

Most often this comes from how the spreadsheet was saved or edited — a stray character, a wrong delimiter, a column that got renamed or dropped, or a value in the wrong format.

How to fix it

You can hunt for it by hand, but the fastest path is to let the tool at the top of this page read the file and point straight at the offending rows or cells — then hand it to your agent to correct in one pass.

Let your agent catch formula and type errors before they ship

In an automated reporting flow, your agent can extract and re-evaluate every formula — catching stripped leading zeros, stale caches, and wrong results before the file reaches a person.

The tools this page runs

Your agent calls xlsx_formulas to extract every formula (cell, formula text, cached result) and xlsx_eval to recompute values live through a real engine — so a stale or missing cache doesn't hand back a wrong number.

  • xlsx_formulas options: sheet, limit, include_results.
  • xlsx_eval options: formulas or cells to evaluate, plus sheet.
