What you get

For every numeric column we show the total, the average, and the range from smallest to largest. For every column — numbers or text — we show how many values it holds, how many are blank, and how many are distinct, with an example value. It’s the fastest way to get your arms around a sheet you didn’t make.

Real totals, not estimates

The totals are summed from the exact cell values, so “total up my data” means an actual sum — not a rounded guess. Everything downloads as a clean .csv you can drop into another sheet.

Your original file is safe

The page reads your file in memory to summarize it, then discards it. Your uploaded .xlsx is never changed and never stored — you download a fresh summary file, so nothing is ever lost.
