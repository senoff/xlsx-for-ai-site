What you get

An exact, cell-by-cell comparison of two workbooks. For every sheet in either file, you see the cells that changed (the value before and after), the cells that were added, and the cells that were removed — plus whole sheets that appeared or disappeared. Download the full diff to keep a record of every change.

Read-only by design

This tool never changes either file. It reads both workbooks in memory, compares them, and discards them — nothing is saved and nothing is written back. The comparison is deterministic: the same two files always produce the same result. It's the same diff your AI agent would get from our API, just rendered for you to read.
