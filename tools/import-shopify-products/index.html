What it does

Shopify's products import expects specific column headers and value formats — Handle, Title, variant option columns, price and inventory fields, and more. Your source list rarely matches them out of the box; the titles, descriptions, prices, and options live under headers of your own. This tool reads your file, maps each column to the right Shopify product field, and writes a clean import CSV you can upload in your Shopify admin.

The mapping ledger is the point

Nothing is mapped silently. Every column you gave us shows up under what we mapped — source header → Shopify field — and anything we couldn't place lands under what needs you, with the reason and how to resolve it. Variant grouping, image assignments, and columns that need a live store connection are flagged there rather than guessed at.

Reviewed file, not an automatic write

This hands you a reviewed import file to check and upload yourself — it never writes to your store directly. You stay in control of what actually lands in Shopify, and your uploaded file is read in memory and never stored.
